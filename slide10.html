The Problem: What Do These Numbers Mean?

A security analyst sees this explanation for the features pushing the prediction towards "ATTACK":

ACK Flag Count = 1.473...
Fwd PSH Flags = 4.535...
Destination Port = -0.439...
Packet Length Mean = -0.540...

These are standardized (scaled) values. They are mathematically correct but operationally unintelligible and useless to a human analyst.
