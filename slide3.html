The Paradox of Modern NIDS

High Accuracy

  • Deep Learning models achieve state-of-the-art performance (>99% F1-score).
  • Excellent at detecting complex, zero-day, and polymorphic attacks.
  • Proven on large-scale, realistic datasets like CIC-IDS-2017.

"Black Box" Nature

  • They provide predictions without transparent reasoning. The "Why?" is missing.
  • An unexplainable alert is of limited value to a Security Operations Center (SOC) analyst.
  • This lack of interpretability erodes trust and hinders practical adoption.

The Core Conflict:

We have powerful detectors, but we can't fully trust or act upon their alerts without understanding their reasoning.
