What SHAP Tells Us

SHAP ranks features by their overall impact on the model's predictions. Higher value means more influential.

The Good News (At First Glance)

The model prioritizes semantically relevant features that a human analyst would also look at:

  • TCP Flags (e.g., ACK, PSH Flag Count)
  • Packet Statistics (e.g., Packet Length Variance)
  • Connection Info (e.g., Destination Port)

A Word of Caution...

While this looks promising, it only tells us WHAT the model uses, not HOW it uses it for a specific case. This is where the "gap" begins to appear.
