Conclusion

The "Last-Mile" Problem is Real

Our analysis confirms that standard application of XAI on preprocessed data results in explanations that are unintelligible and lack practical utility for security analysts.

Post-Processing is the Bridge

We demonstrated that essential post-processing steps, like inverse transformation and contextualization, are required to translate raw XAI outputs into actionable, human-readable insights.

Final Message: Mind the Gap

To unlock the true potential of XAI in cybersecurity, the community must move beyond theoretical explainability and focus on building truly practical, human-centric interpretation workflows.
